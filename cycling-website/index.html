CYCLING IS GOOD FOR YOU

You can park your bicycle at the front of the lot and avoid paying tolls when you reach your destination. It will be difficult to notice you are exercising when you are cycling with your family members. Cycling increases your child's fitness awareness as well as prevents a child from becoming overweight. Children are kept active by riding bikes. For children to develop strong, healthy bones and muscles, most health experts recommend an hour of physical activity daily.
